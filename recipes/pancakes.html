Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Oatmeal
  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 3 tbsp Sugar
  • 3 tsp Baking Powder
  • 2 cups Milk
  • 3 Eggs
  • 3 tbsp Butter
  • Chocolate Chips (Optional)
    •

    Directions

    1. Start by adding the dry ingredients into a large bowl. Stir mixture to combine.
    2. Next add the wet ingredients into a seperate bowl and wisk to combine.
    3. Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients and use a spatula or a large spoon the fold the ingredients together. Be carful not to over-mix the batter as it can make the pancakes thinner when cooking.
    4. Use a flat pan and use a meduim heat setting to warm up the pan.
    5. Once the batter is ready and the pan is at a medium temperature, use butter to grease the pan before adding any batter otherwise it will stick
    6. Use 1/4 cups of batter per pancake. (Note: Make sure not to over crowd the pan and try to be effiencnt with the space in the pan.)
    7. Optional: Sprinkle chockolate chips over the pancakes and check to makesure they settle into the batter. This should be done immediatly after adding the batter into the pan
    8. Wait for the top to start bubbling and then flip the pancakes. Cook for two to three more minutes on the otherside and they will be ready to serve. (Note: Add more butter to the pan before making another round of pancakes.)
    9. Serve with syrip and a side of fruit. Enjoy!
