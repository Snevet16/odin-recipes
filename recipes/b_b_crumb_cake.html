Blueberry Crumb Cake

Ingredients

  • 3-1/2 cups Flour
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 3 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1-1/2 cups butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 2 cups blueberries
    •

      Directions

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 Degrees.
    2. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, sugar, salt and baking powder.
    3. Next, add 3/4 cups of butter and add use your hand to mix the butter with the mixture like a pie crust. (Pinch the butter between the fingertips to combine with dry ingredients.)
    4. Take out 2-1/2 cups of the mixture and set aside.
    5. Crack two eggs into a 1 cup measure and fill to the rest of the space with milk and add to the original mixture.
    6. Add the blueberries and fold in with a spatula.
      7. Grease a 9x12 pan and fill mith the mixture.
    7. Now for the mixture set aside. Add another 3/4 cups of melter butter and the cinnamon. combine.
    8. Sprinkle the crumbly mixture on top of the mixture in the pan and bake for 45 minutes or until golden brown.
    9. Take out of the oven and leave to cool for an hour before serving.
