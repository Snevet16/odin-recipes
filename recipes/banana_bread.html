Banana Bread (Oatmeal)

    Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups Oats
  • 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour
  • 1-1/2 cups Mashed Ripe Bananas
  • 2/3 cup Honey
  • 3 tbsp Greek Yogurt
  • 4 tbsp Unsalted Butter
  • 1/2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/4 cup Milk
  • 2 Eggs
    • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/4 tsp Salt

    Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix the dry ingredients together and wisk to combine.
  3. In a larger bowl, Mash the ripe bananas. It should be about 2-1/2 to 3 bananas.
  4. Next, wisk in the honey, butter, milk, eggs, and Greek yogurt.
  5. Add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients and mix with a rubber spatual until the mixture is homageneous.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes then loosely cover the pan with aluminum foil and continue to cook for an additional 35-45 minutes. (65-75 total cooking time.)
  7. Place pan on a cooling rack and let rest for 1 hour before serving.
